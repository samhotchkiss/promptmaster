# 0006 Control Room Polish And Validation

## Goal

Keep iterating on the Prompt Master control room until it feels faster, clearer, and more intentional in tmux.

## Scope

- Continue live tmux validation of the Accounts, Projects, Sessions, Alerts, and Events tabs.
- Fix rough edges in keyboard/mouse interaction, layout clarity, and status messaging.
- Reduce confusion around what the operator should click or do next.

## Acceptance Criteria

- No known dead-end actions in the control room.
- Common actions have visible labels and stable focus behavior.
- Overnight tmux smoke tests leave the control plane in a clean state.
