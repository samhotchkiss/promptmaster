# 0017 Onboarding Wording And Project Discovery Polish

## Goal

Tighten the first-run PollyPM flow so onboarding copy, project discovery prompts, and the handoff into Polly feel crisp, intentional, and aligned with the product direction.

## Scope

- refine onboarding wording and sequencing
- improve project discovery suggestions and explanation text
- make the transition from setup into Polly feel smooth and explicit
- keep the copy aligned with PollyPM / Polly naming

## Acceptance Criteria

- onboarding copy feels product-grade rather than scaffold-like
- recent-project suggestions are clearly explained and easy to act on
- the end of onboarding hands off into Polly without confusion
